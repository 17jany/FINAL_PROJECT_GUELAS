Cybersecurity Tips

Software Updates

Keeping your software up to date is crucial to protecting against the latest threats and vulnerabilities.

  • Enable automatic updates whenever possible.
  • Regularly check for updates for your operating system, browsers, and applications.
  • Avoid using outdated or unsupported software.
  • Use trusted sources for downloading updates.

Phishing Awareness

Phishing attacks aim to trick users into revealing personal or sensitive information. Stay alert with these tips:

  • Be cautious with emails from unknown senders.
  • Look for spelling and grammar mistakes in suspicious messages.
  • Never click on suspicious links or attachments.
  • Verify the URL before logging in to any website.
  • Report phishing attempts to your IT department or provider.

Safe Browsing Practices

Browsing the internet safely helps prevent malware infections and data theft. Here are a few reminders:

  • Use a secure and updated browser.
  • Install ad blockers and antivirus plugins.
  • Look for HTTPS before entering sensitive information.
  • Clear your cache and cookies regularly.
  • Avoid using public Wi-Fi for transactions or logins.
